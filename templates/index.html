NEW EYES

        This website is designed to assist visually impaired individuals in learning conveniently through electronic devices such as smartphones or tablets. The website operates by utilizing the deviceâ€™s camera to detect text, converting the text captured from documents, books, or signs into digital data. The extracted text is then processed to ensure accuracy and suitability for further use. Once the text has been processed, the website employs text-to-speech technology to read the text aloud to the user. Users can listen to the text in real time simply by pointing the camera at the desired text.
